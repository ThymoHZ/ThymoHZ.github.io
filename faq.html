FAQ

Here are the most Frequently asked questions about the HZ

These question with the anwser you will find here, would you have more question feel free to submit your questions below.

  1. How can you print a document from you laptop at HZ?
  2. How can you scan a document and sent it to your laptop at HZ?
  3. How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?
  4. How can you book a project space?
  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Here are the anwsers for the 5 most frequent questions we ave recieved.

  1. First you find a printer and make sure you send the document you like to have print is send to designated printer, after you can go to that printer and with you school pas you can get the document you have send over printed out.
  2. Firstly scan the document on a printer in the HZ building put it on your school pas and request to recieve the scan on your laptop from the printer.
  3. You go to the online webshop at HZ and login with your HZ details and then you will be able to make purchases on the website.
  4. On myHZ webpage you can find the in the left bracket you can add the button for request a room, once you click there you will be send to a different page. On that page you will see wich rooms are when available and when certain room are already booked. further more you can also filter by etage and also on what you need in the room for example a beamer.
  5. If you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot you need to make sure you have parked in the correct lot, once you parked during the day you can visit the JRCZ building and you can be handed a ticket so you can get off the lot without having to pay.
