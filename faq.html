FAQ

Here are the most Frequently asked questions about the HZ

These question with the anwser you will find here, would you have more question feel free to submit your questions below.

  1. How can you print a document from you laptop at HZ?
  2. How can you scan a document and sent it to your laptop at HZ?
  3. How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?
  4. How can you book a project space?
  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Here are the anwsers for the 5 most frequent questions we ave recieved.

  1. In the desired application, choose print/print and select the HZ_Printer. When the print command is given, log on to the printer by entering your HZ card or username/password. On the printer, press Print Release. After printing, press the following symbol on the printer to log off:
  2. Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ user name & password. Choose the Scan option in the menu. Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate. Double-sided scanning (optional). Press Change settings. For duplex mode, select the 2-sided option. Press Start. After scanning, press the following symbol on the printer to log off:
  3. Place your order via the selfservice Portal on MyHZ. That way, you will be helped quickly in the HZ Shop and your order will be processed with care. It is not possible to place your order via e-mail.
  4. On myHZ webpage you can find the in the left bracket you can add the button for request a room, once you click there you will be send to a different page. On that page you will see wich rooms are when available and when certain room are already booked. further more you can also filter by etage and also on what you need in the room for example a beamer.
  5. If you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot you need to make sure you have parked in the kousteensedijk parking lot. Upon presentation of your entry card, you can obtain an exit card at the reception of Het Groene Woud. With this card, you can exit for free.
